Hike & Go Co. is a supplier of camping and outdoor equipment. We sell all items at a discount to provide the best prices for our customers. We look to satisfy 100% of all potential and returning customers.
We also provide hiking and outdoor trips all across North America. Whether traveling solo or in a group. We look for the most exciting and majestic places to explore.
We looked around and saw outrageous prices for equipment that was marked up close to 300% of what they should be and decided it shouldn't be like that. We wanted to change that and make sure more people were getting out exploring, not letting finances get in the way of that.
We love taking trips and exploring new places. Why wouldn't we want to share that? We try to make it as affordable and unique as possible. Whether an amateur adventurer or an expert explorer, we try to help find the right trip just for you.
Please contact us with any issues or questions you may have. We strive to provide all reasonable accommodations to give you your money back if you aren't completely satisfied.
